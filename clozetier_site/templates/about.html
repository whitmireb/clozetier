WHAT IS CLOZETIER?

Clozetier is a smart, AI-powered clothing organization and recommendation platform designed to help you organize, style, and make the most of your wardrobe. By uploading photos of your clothing, Clozetier can categorize and store them for easy access. It also suggests outfit pairings, making it simple to find matching pieces based on color and style, giving you confidence in your everyday wardrobe choices!

CORE FEATURES

  • Clothing Upload & Categorization: Clozetier lets you upload photos of your clothes, which are automatically categorized using AI. Each item is classified by both color and type of clothing, making it easy to filter and find specific items in your wardrobe.
  • Outfit Recommendations: Struggling to decide what to wear? Clozetier’s AI recommendation system analyzes your closet’s contents and suggests clothing combinations that match well together, helping you build cohesive outfits with ease.
  • Personalized Closet Database: All your clothing items are stored in your personal digital "closet" on the platform. Each item is saved with its classification data, so you can view and organize your wardrobe at a glance.
  • Account & User Interface: The interface is designed for easy navigation with a side tab for quick access to your account, closet, outfit recommendations, and the about page.

HOW TO USE CLOZETIER

  1. Upload Your Clothing Photos: Start by uploading clear images of each clothing item in your wardrobe. Clozetier’s AI will automatically detect and classify each item by type and color.
  2. Organize Your Digital Closet: As you upload, Clozetier builds a comprehensive digital closet for you, where you can view, filter, and organize your wardrobe.
  3. Get Outfit Suggestions: Select any item in your closet, and Clozetier will suggest complementary pieces to create a coordinated outfit.
  4. Manage & Customize Your Wardrobe: Add, remove, and update items as your wardrobe changes, ensuring Clozetier always has an accurate inventory to pull from.
