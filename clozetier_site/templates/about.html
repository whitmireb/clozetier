What is Clozetier?

Clozetier is a smart, AI-powered clothing organization and recommendation platform designed to help you organize, style, and make the most of your wardrobe. By uploading photos of your clothing, Clozetier can categorize and store them for easy access. It also suggests outfit pairings, making it simple to find matching pieces based on color and style, giving you confidence in your everyday wardrobe choices!

Core Features

  • Clothing Upload & Categorization: Upload photos of your clothes, which are automatically categorized using AI.
  • Outfit Recommendations: AI suggests clothing combinations that match well together.
  • Personalized Closet Database: All your clothing items are stored in your personal digital "closet".
  • Account & User Interface: Easy navigation with a sidebar for quick access.

How to Use Clozetier

  1. Upload your clothing photos.
  2. Organize your digital closet.
  3. Get outfit suggestions.
  4. Manage and customize your wardrobe.

Our Mission

At Clozetier, our mission is to make styling and organizing clothing easier and more intuitive. We believe everyone deserves confidence in their everyday style choices.
